What happens in a session?

Each lab is designed to feel like a mix between a strategy room and a game table:

  • Warm-up puzzles to get brains firing.
  • Targeted concept work (fractions, place value, word problems, etc.).
  • High-quality games that build mental flexibility and focus.
  • Short reflection: “What did we learn? What got easier?”
Number flexibility Problem solving Pattern recognition Spatial reasoning

Why it matters

More than math scores — but scores matter, too.

You care about test results — MAP, SOL, report cards. So do we. But we also care about the kind of thinker your child is becoming.

From “getting by” to leading the pack.

Many students can “do the steps” in math but don’t truly understand what they’re doing. In the lab, we:

  • Press for explanations, not just answers.
  • Build stamina with multi-step problems.
  • Celebrate struggle as part of learning.
  • Connect what we do to what they’ll see in class and on tests.

Fun is a strategy, not a distraction.

Engagement isn’t the opposite of rigor. When kids are genuinely engaged, they:

  • Spend more time thinking deeply.
  • Retain strategies longer.
  • Take more academic risks — a big predictor of growth.

That’s why we combine games, puzzles, and coaching — not to entertain, but to build serious skill in a way kids actually love.
